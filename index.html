Husky Bean Coffee

Manhattan, KS

Husky Bean Coffee is an online coffee shop consisting of different aromas and roasts. Only the finest quality,
which has been handpicked, with 100% Specialty Grade. We take pride in what we sell and aim to make
the process easy for you.

Our Mission

  • Husky Bean Coffee aims to make a positive impact on those who purchase by selling the world's finest
    grade coffee.
  • We will donate 5% of all profits to local and nationwide dog shelters in need.
  • Our purpose is to bring attention to the increasing demand for dogs without families.
